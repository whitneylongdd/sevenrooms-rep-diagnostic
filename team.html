All metrics are calculated from two sources: the 2026 Rep Productivity Tracker Google Sheet (deal-level data) and the Gong Scorecard (call quality scores). The dashboard covers NAM AE and AM reps with activity in 2026 (Jan 1 – May 12). Filters apply to the Dashboard tab only.

📦 Data Sources

Raw Data – Opps CW
Deal-level closed-won opportunity data from the 2026 Rep Productivity Tracker. ~11,900 rows. Used for CW counts, MRR, facecard segmentation, and monthly trends. Filtered to NAM geo, AE/AM roles, Jan–May 2026.
Source: 2026 Rep Productivity Tracker → "Raw Data - Opps CW" tab
Raw Data – Opps Created
Deal-level SQO (Sales Qualified Opportunity) creation data. ~26,400 rows. Used solely to calculate SQO counts per rep per month. Filtered to NAM geo, Jan–May 2026.
Source: 2026 Rep Productivity Tracker → "Raw Data - Opps Created" tab
Gong Scorecard
Call quality scores for reps with scored Gong calls. Covers Feb 10 – May 11 2026. Includes an overall score and 8 sub-dimension scores on a 1–5 scale (Agenda is 0–1). Not all reps have Gong data.
Source: Gong Scorecard export (previously shared)

📊 Summary Bar Metrics

Total CW 2026
SUM(cw2026) across filtered reps
Total closed-won deal count for all filtered reps in 2026 YTD. Updates when Q1/Q2 filter is applied — Q1 uses Jan+Feb+Mar only, Q2 uses Apr+May only.
Source: Raw Data – Opps CW · Column: Opp CW Month
Won MRR 2026
SUM(mrr_final) across filtered reps
Total monthly recurring revenue from closed-won deals. When a quarter filter is active, MRR is estimated proportionally: (quarter CW / total CW) × total MRR per rep.
Source: Raw Data – Opps CW · Column: MRR Final
Avg CVR
AVG(cw2026 / sqo2026) across reps with SQOs
Average Conversion Rate across all filtered reps who have at least one SQO in 2026. Reps with zero SQOs are excluded from this average. Color-coded: green ≥30%, amber 20–29%, red <20%.
Source: Opps CW + Opps Created tabs
Avg Gong
AVG(gong_overall) across reps with scored calls
Average overall Gong call quality score (1–5 scale) across reps who have at least one scored call in the Gong data. Reps without Gong data are excluded.
Source: Gong Scorecard export
Active Reps
COUNT(reps where CW > 0)
Number of reps in the filtered view with at least one closed-won deal in the selected period. Updates with quarter filter.
Source: Raw Data – Opps CW
Jack Mix %
SUM(jack_cw) / SUM(total_cw)
Percentage of all closed-won deals that are Jack-segment customers. Jack = mid-market restaurant accounts. High Jack mix can indicate reps are avoiding harder Ace/King deals.
Source: Raw Data – Opps CW · Column: Facecard Final

🃏 Facecard Segmentation

Facecard is SevenRooms' internal account segmentation. Every closed-won deal is tagged with a facecard based on the customer profile.
Ace
Enterprise / high-value accounts. Typically larger groups, multi-location operators, or flagship brands. Highest ACV potential. Longer sales cycles.
Source: Column "Facecard Final" = "Ace"
Jack
Mid-market accounts. The majority of deal volume. Represents the core of NAM SMB motion. High-volume, moderate ACV.
Source: Column "Facecard Final" = "Jack"
King
Upmarket SMB or boutique high-value accounts. Higher ACV than Jack, lower than Ace. Strategic segment for expansion-led growth.
Source: Column "Facecard Final" = "King"

👤 Rep Card Metrics

CW (Closed Won)
COUNT(deals where Opp CW Month in period)
Total closed-won deals for the rep in the selected period. Q1 = Jan–Mar, Q2 = Apr–May, Full Year = Jan–May.
Source: Raw Data – Opps CW
Net New
COUNT(deals where type = New)
Closed-won deals that are new logo or new subscription, excluding expansion/upsell. Only shown for Full Year view — not broken out by quarter in current data.
Source: Column "Subscription Item Opp Type Detail Category"
SQOs
COUNT(opps created in period)
Sales Qualified Opportunities created by the rep in 2026 YTD. Used as the denominator in CVR. Note: SQOs created in one period may close in a future period, so CVR is a pipeline efficiency signal, not a strict rate.
Source: Raw Data – Opps Created · Column: Month of Opp SQO
MRR
SUM(MRR Final) for closed-won deals
Monthly recurring revenue from closed-won deals in the period. When a quarter is selected, MRR is estimated proportionally based on that quarter's share of total CW deals.
Source: Raw Data – Opps CW · Column: MRR Final
CVR (Conversion Rate)
cw2026 / sqo2026 × 100
Percentage of SQOs that converted to closed-won. Tiers: Elite ≥45%, Solid 30–44%, At Risk 20–29%, Critical <20%. Reps with 0 SQOs show no CVR. CVR >100% is possible if prior-year SQOs close in 2026.
Source: Opps CW + Opps Created, 2026 YTD
Ramp Status
Ramp at time of opp closed won < 1 → Ramping
Whether the rep was fully ramped during 2026. A "Ramping" chip appears if the rep's ramp factor was below 1.0 for their closed deals. Ramp factor comes from the spreadsheet and reflects time-in-seat productivity expectations.
Source: Column "Ramp at time of opp closed won"

🎙 Gong Scorecard Dimensions

Gong scores are averages across all scored calls for each rep during Feb 10 – May 11, 2026. All dimensions are 1–5 scale except Agenda (0–1). The scoring rubric below reflects the criteria used by Gong reviewers.
Overall Gong Score
AVG of all scored sub-dimensions
Composite call quality score. Threshold for "good": ≥3.5. Color coding: green ≥80% of max, blue 60–79%, amber 40–59%, red <40%.
Research
1–5 scale
Did the rep demonstrate pre-call research? Scored on evidence of account knowledge, personalization, and relevance of opening.
Agenda
0–1 scale (Yes/No)
Did the rep set a clear agenda at the start of the call? Binary score — either present (1) or absent (0). Average across calls gives a rate.
Discovery
1–5 scale
Quality of discovery questions asked. Evaluates depth of questioning, whether the rep uncovered needs, and how well they listened vs. pitched.
Pain
1–5 scale
Did the rep identify and quantify the prospect's pain points? Measures ability to surface business problems and tie them to impact.
Goals
1–5 scale
Did the rep connect the solution to the prospect's stated business goals? Evaluates strategic alignment and value framing.
Solution
1–5 scale
Quality of solution presentation. Scored on relevance, clarity, and whether the demo/pitch was tailored to the discovered needs.
Objection Handling
1–5 scale
How effectively did the rep handle objections? Evaluates acknowledgment, reframing, and resolution of prospect concerns.
Next Steps
1–5 scale
Did the rep secure clear, committed next steps by end of call? Evaluates specificity (date, attendees, action) and mutual commitment.

⚠️ Known Limitations

CVR > 100%
Some reps show conversion rates above 100%. This happens when deals close from SQOs created before 2026. CVR is a pipeline efficiency signal, not a strict closed/created rate.
May Data is Partial
May 2026 data runs through May 12 only. May CW and MRR figures will be lower than a full month. The trend note calls this out explicitly.
Quarter MRR is Estimated
When Q1 or Q2 is selected, per-rep MRR is estimated as (quarter CW / total CW) × total MRR. This assumes uniform MRR per deal, which may not be exact.
Gong Coverage is Partial
Not all reps have Gong scores. Reps hired recently, those who don't use Gong, or those with fewer than a threshold of scored calls may show "No Gong" on their card.
SQO Timing Mismatch
SQOs are counted by creation date; CW by close date. A rep with high SQOs in Jan may not see those close until Mar/Apr. CVR should be interpreted as a lagging signal.
Region Gaps
Some reps show "–" for region. These are typically AMs or reps not yet assigned to a region in the tracker. They appear when "All" is selected but are excluded from region filters.
