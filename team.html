What does all this mean?
Key themes · Benchmarks · Enablement cohorts  ·  66 scored reps, 93 total
Critical Gap
Only 11% of calls open with an agenda
Agenda Setting is scored Yes/No — and across all 66 scored reps almost no one states what will be covered, gives a time estimate, and confirms the prospect agrees before launching in. This is the single most fixable habit on the team.
Systemic Pattern
The team pitches before it discovers
Discovery averages 2.0/5, Goals & Aspirations 1.5/5 — both below the developing threshold. Reps are reaching prospects but defaulting to demos before understanding the business. Scores of 1–2 on discovery = mostly closed-ended or rhetorical questions.
Pipeline Reality
22 of 75 fully-ramped reps have zero Q2 wins
That's 29% of ramped reps with nothing closed this quarter. Top performers show 16 and 16 wins — the gap between top and bottom is large and not explained by ramp status alone.

Session 1 · "Open the Call Right"
Universal — all 66 scored reps · Agenda Setting
Only 11% of calls start with a structured agenda. The fix is a 30-second script: state 3 things you'll cover, give a time estimate, ask "does that work?" This primes the prospect for a conversation, not a pitch — and it's the most coachable habit on the team.
